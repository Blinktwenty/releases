## What is Blink20?

Blink20 is a free eye health assistant that helps you fight digital eye strain by turning the optometrist-backed **20-20-20 rule** into an effortless daily habit:

> Every **20 minutes**, look at something **20 feet away**, for at least **20 seconds**.

Prolonged screen time reduces your blink rate and keeps your eye muscles locked at a fixed focal distance, which leads to dryness, fatigue, blurred vision, and headaches — commonly known as *computer vision syndrome*. Blink20 sits quietly alongside your work and guides well-timed micro-breaks so your eyes get the recovery they need without breaking your flow.

## Key Features

- **Smart break reminders** — non-intrusive, well-timed prompts built around the 20-20-20 principle, tuned to real work sessions rather than rigid timers.
- **Personalized timing** — adapt break cadence to your own rhythm, workload, and schedule.
- **Calming soundscapes** — rest your eyes to ambient audio (forest rain, focus tones, and more) during each break.
- **Vision insights** — weekly trends, streaks, and milestones that quantify your break habits over time.
- **On-device AI** — all detection and analysis run locally on your machine.

## Privacy by Design

Blink20 is built privacy-first:

- **No accounts.** You never sign up or log in.
- **No data collection.** Nothing is uploaded, tracked, or sold — ever.
- **No internet required.** Every feature works fully offline; all processing happens on your device.

Read the full policy at [blink20.com/privacy-policy](https://blink20.com/privacy-policy).

### Installing on macOS

1. Download the latest `.dmg` from the [Releases page](https://github.com/Blinktwenty/releases/releases/latest).
2. Open the `.dmg` and drag **Blink20** into your **Applications** folder.
3. On first launch, macOS may warn that the app is from an unidentified developer. Right-click the app → **Open** → **Open** to confirm.
